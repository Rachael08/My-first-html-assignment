MY MOTIVATION TO BEING A WEB DEVELOPER

I have always wanted to know one or two things in the IT world. Getting to be part of the CapacityBay Family is a privilege I do not take for granted. During the course of this program, I have experienced to an extent how challenging, functional and creative web development can be. Getting to know this about web development has fueled my interest in building tech skills. Web development is artistic and beautiful. It has given me a chance to express my creativity in the technical world. Web development is poised for continued growth and innovation.

I look forward to when I'll be a professional web developer and also learn more functional skill that will make me relevant in the IT world. Five years from now, I'll love to be among programmers that creates websites and mobile apps, develop complex software systems whether in business, healthcare, education, or any other field. I will love to experience in full the many growth opportunities programming has to offer. I look forward to collaborating with people from all sorts of backgrounds and cultures, broaden my horizons and enhance my creativity, learn about new technologies and techniques.

BENEFITS OF BEING A WEB DEVELOPER

  1. High creative potential
  2. Ability to work in various work environments
  3. Opportunity for innovation and invention.
  4. Freelance opportunities.
  5. Short education time.
  6. Career growth
  7. Chance to learn transferable skills
  8. Being a web developer is a universal skill
  9. Lots of opportunity to collaborate and pick up new skills
  10. It is really fun!

CONS OF BEING A WEB DEVELOPER

  • Includes long hours
  • Requires consistent changes to meet basic practice guidelines
  • Requires teamwork
  • Involves a high-pressure environment
    • It may require tight deadlines and challenging delivery schedules
  • Requires coding knowledge
    • This process is time-consuming and difficult to master
